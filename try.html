The Fall Guy

The summer blockbuster season kicks off with a love letter to the art of stunt performances. Hollywood darlings Ryan Gosling and Emily Blunt star in this big old action flick that decorates its classic hero's journey plot in the most lavish stunts you'll see on screen this year - including driver Logan Holladay's world-recording shattering 8 1/2 rotation cannon roll in a modified Jeep Grand Cherokee. Be prepared to gain a whole new appreciation for stunt work from director David Leitch (John Wick, Atomic Blonde) who started his career as a stunt performer himself, notably as Brad Pitt's double back in the Fight Club years. And keep an eye out for Lee Majors, who starred in the original '80s TV series and is rumored to make an on-screen appearance.
